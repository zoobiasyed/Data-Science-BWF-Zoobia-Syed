The "Defining Data Science" section of Microsoft's Data Science for Beginners course explains what data science is, its importance, and its key components. The "Ethics in Data Science" section of Microsoft's Data Science for Beginners course addresses the importance of ethical considerations in data science. It covers topics such as bias in data, privacy concerns, and the responsible use of data. The section emphasizes the need for transparency, accountability, and ethical decision-making to ensure that data science practices do not harm individuals or society.
